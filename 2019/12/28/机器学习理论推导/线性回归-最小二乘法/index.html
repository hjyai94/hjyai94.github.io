线性回归-最小二乘法

发表于 2019-12-28 | 分类于 学习

D={(x1,y1),(x2,y2),⋯,(xN,yN)}xi∈Rp,yi∈R,i=1,2,⋯,N\begin{aligned} &D=\left\lbrace(x_1, y_1),\left(x_{2}, y_{2}\right), \cdots,\left(x_{N}, y_{N}\right)\right\rbrace \\\\ &x_{i} \in \mathbb{R}^{p}, \quad y_{i} \in \mathbb{R}, \quad i=1,2, \cdots, N \end{aligned}​D={(x1​,y1​),(x2​,y2​),⋯,(xN​,yN​)}xi​∈Rp,yi​∈R,i=1,2,⋯,N​

X=(x1x2⋯xN)T=(x11x12⋯x1px21x22⋯x2p⋮⋮⋱⋮xN1xN2⋯xNp)X = \begin{pmatrix} {x_{1}}&{x_{2}}&{\cdots}&{x_{N}} \end{pmatrix}^T = \begin{pmatrix} {x_{11}}&{x_{12}}&{\cdots}&{x_{1p}}\\\\ {x_{21}}&{x_{22}}&{\cdots}&{x_{2p}}\\\\ {\vdots}&{\vdots}&{\ddots}&{\vdots}\\\\ {x_{N1}}&{x_{N2}}&{\cdots}&{x_{Np}}\\\\ \end{pmatrix}X=(x1​​x2​​⋯​xN​​)T=⎝⎜⎜⎜⎜⎜⎜⎜⎜⎜⎜⎛​x11​x21​⋮xN1​​x12​x22​⋮xN2​​⋯⋯⋱⋯​x1p​x2p​⋮xNp​​⎠⎟⎟⎟⎟⎟⎟⎟⎟⎟⎟⎞​

Y=(y1y2⋮yN)N×1Y=\left(\begin{array}{c} {y_{1}} \\\\ {y_{2}} \\\\ {\vdots} \\\\ {y_{N}} \end{array}\right)_{N \times 1} Y=⎝⎜⎜⎜⎜⎜⎜⎜⎜⎛​y1​y2​⋮yN​​⎠⎟⎟⎟⎟⎟⎟⎟⎟⎞​N×1​

最小二乘估计：

L(w)=(∑i=1N∥∣w⊤xi−yi∥∣2)=∑i=1N(w⊤xi−yi)2=(w⊤x1−y1w⊤x2−y2⋯w⊤xN−yN)(w⊤x1−y1w⊤x2−y2⋮w⊤xN−yN)=(w⊤X⊤−Y⊤)(Xw−Y)=wTX⊤Xw−2w⊤X⊤Y+Y⊤Y\begin{aligned} L(w) &=\left(\sum_{i=1}^{N}\left\|\left|w^{\top} x_{i}-y_{i}\right\|\right|^{2}\right) \\\\ &=\sum_{i=1}^{N}\left(w^{\top} x_{i}-y_{i}\right)^{2} \\\\ &=\left( w^{\top} x_{1}-y_{1} \quad w^{\top} x_{2}-y_{2} \quad \cdots \quad w^{\top} x_{N}-y_{N} \right) \left(\begin{array}{c} {w^{\top} x_{1}-y_{1}} \\\\ {w^{\top} x_{2}-y_{2}} \\\\ {\vdots} \\\\ {w^{\top} x_{N}-y_{N}} \end{array}\right) \\\\ &= \left(w^{\top} X^{\top} - Y^{\top} \right) \left(Xw - Y \right) \\\\ &= w^{T} {X}^{\top} X w-2 w^{\top} X^{\top} Y+Y^{\top} Y \end{aligned} L(w)​=(i=1∑N​∥∥​∣∣​w⊤xi​−yi​∥∥​∣∣​2)=i=1∑N​(w⊤xi​−yi​)2=(w⊤x1​−y1​w⊤x2​−y2​⋯w⊤xN​−yN​)⎝⎜⎜⎜⎜⎜⎜⎜⎜⎛​w⊤x1​−y1​w⊤x2​−y2​⋮w⊤xN​−yN​​⎠⎟⎟⎟⎟⎟⎟⎟⎟⎞​=(w⊤X⊤−Y⊤)(Xw−Y)=wTX⊤Xw−2w⊤X⊤Y+Y⊤Y​

对上式求导，并令其为000：

\hat{w}=\arg \min L(w) $$ $$\frac{\partial L(w)}{\partial w}=2 X^{\top} X w-2 X^{\top} Y = 0$$ $$ \hat{w} = \left( X^{\top} X \right)^{-1} X^{\top} Y
