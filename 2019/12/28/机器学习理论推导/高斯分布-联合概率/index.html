高斯分布-联合概率

发表于 2019-12-28 | 分类于 学习

已知：$$p(x)=N\left(x | \mu, \Lambda^{-1}\right)$$ $$p(y | x)=N\left(y | A x+b, L^{-1}\right)$$
求：p(y)p(y)p(y)，p(x∣y)p(x|y)p(x∣y)

y=Ax+b+εε∼N(0,L−1)\begin{aligned} &y=A x+b+\varepsilon\\\\ &\varepsilon \sim N\left(0, L^{-1}\right) \end{aligned} ​y=Ax+b+εε∼N(0,L−1)​

E[y]=E[Ax+b+ε]=E[Ax+b]+E[ε]=Aμ+b\begin{aligned} E[y] &=E[A x+b+\varepsilon]=E[A x+b]+E[\varepsilon] \\\\ &=A \mu+b \end{aligned} E[y]​=E[Ax+b+ε]=E[Ax+b]+E[ε]=Aμ+b​

Var⁡[y]=Var⁡[Ax+b+ε]=Var⁡[Ax+b]+Var⁡[ε]=AΛ−1A⊤+L−1\operatorname{Var}[y]=\operatorname{Var}[A x+b+\varepsilon]= \operatorname{Var}[A x+b]+\operatorname{Var} [\varepsilon] =A \Lambda^{-1} A^{\top}+L^{-1} Var[y]=Var[Ax+b+ε]=Var[Ax+b]+Var[ε]=AΛ−1A⊤+L−1

y∼N(Aμ+b,L−1+AΛ−1A⊤)y \sim N\left(A \mu+b, L^{-1}+A \Lambda^{-1} A^{\top}\right) y∼N(Aμ+b,L−1+AΛ−1A⊤)

z=(xy)∼N([μAμ+b],[λ−1ΔΔTL−1+AΛ−1AT])z=\left(\begin{array}{c} {x} \\\\ {y} \end{array}\right) \sim N\left(\left[\begin{array}{c} {\mu} \\\\ {A \mu+b} \end{array}\right],\left[\begin{array}{cc} {\lambda^{-1}} & {\Delta} \\\\ {\Delta^T} & {L^{-1}+A \Lambda^{-1} A^{T}} \end{array}\right]\right) z=⎝⎛​xy​⎠⎞​∼N⎝⎛​⎣⎡​μAμ+b​⎦⎤​,⎣⎡​λ−1ΔT​ΔL−1+AΛ−1AT​⎦⎤​⎠⎞​

直接套用上一篇博客条件概率公式，变可以得出p(x∣y)p(x|y)p(x∣y)。
下面求Δ\DeltaΔ的值：

Δ=Cov⁡(x,y)=E[(x−E[x])⋅(y−E[y])T]=E[(x−μ)(y−Aμ−b)T]=E[(x−μ)(Ax−Aμ+ε)T]=E[(x−μ)(Ax−Aμ+ε)T]=E[(x−μ)(Ax−Aμ)T]+E[(x−μ)εT]=E[(x−μ)(Ax−Aμ)T]+E[(x−μ)εT]=E[(x−μ)(Ax−Aμ)T]=E[(x−μ)(x−μ)T⋅AT]=E[(x−μ)(x−μ)T]⋅AT=Var⁡[x]⋅AT=Λ−1AT\begin{aligned} \Delta &=\operatorname{Cov}(x, y) \\\\ &=E[(x-E[x]) \cdot(y-E[y])^{T}] \\\\ &=E[(x-\mu)(y-A \mu-b)^{T}] \\\\ &=E\left[(x-\mu)({A x-A \mu}+\varepsilon)^{T}\right] \\\\ &=E\left[(x-\mu)({A x-A \mu}+\varepsilon)^{T}\right] \\\\ &=E\left[(x-\mu)(A x-A\mu)^{T}\right]+E\left[(x-\mu) \varepsilon^{T}\right] \\\\ &=E\left[(x-\mu)(A x-A\mu)^{T}\right]+E\left[(x-\mu) \varepsilon^{T}\right] \\\\ &=E\left[(x-\mu)(A x-A \mu)^{T}\right]\\\\ &=E\left[(x-\mu)(x-\mu)^{T} \cdot A^{T}\right]\\\\ &\begin{array}{l} {=E\left[(x-\mu)(x-\mu)^{T}\right] \cdot A^{T}} \\\\ {=\operatorname{Var}[x] \cdot A^{T}} \end{array}\\\\ &=\Lambda^{-1} A^{T} \end{aligned} Δ​=Cov(x,y)=E[(x−E[x])⋅(y−E[y])T]=E[(x−μ)(y−Aμ−b)T]=E[(x−μ)(Ax−Aμ+ε)T]=E[(x−μ)(Ax−Aμ+ε)T]=E[(x−μ)(Ax−Aμ)T]+E[(x−μ)εT]=E[(x−μ)(Ax−Aμ)T]+E[(x−μ)εT]=E[(x−μ)(Ax−Aμ)T]=E[(x−μ)(x−μ)T⋅AT]=E[(x−μ)(x−μ)T]⋅AT=Var[x]⋅AT​=Λ−1AT​
