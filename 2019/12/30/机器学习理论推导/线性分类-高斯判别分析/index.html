定义

{(xi,yi)}i=1Nxi∈Rp,yi∈{0,1}\begin{aligned} &\lbrace\left(x_{i}, y_{i}\right)\rbrace_{i=1}^{N}\\\\ &x_{i} \in \mathbb{R}^{p}, \quad y_{i} \in \lbrace 0,1\rbrace \end{aligned} ​{(xi​,yi​)}i=1N​xi​∈Rp,yi​∈{0,1}​

C1={xi∣yi=1,i=1,⋯,N}→S1C2={xi∣yi=0,i=1,⋯,N}→S2∣C1∣=N1,∣C2∣=N2,N1+N2=N\begin{aligned} &C_{1}=\lbrace x_{i} | y_{i}=1, i=1, \cdots, N\rbrace \rightarrow S_{1}\\\\ &C_{2}=\lbrace x_{i} | y_{i}=0, i=1, \cdots, N\rbrace \rightarrow S_{2}\\\\ &\left|C_{1}\right|=N_{1}, \quad\left|C_{2}\right|=N_{2}, \quad N_{1}+N_{2}=N \end{aligned} ​C1​={xi​∣yi​=1,i=1,⋯,N}→S1​C2​={xi​∣yi​=0,i=1,⋯,N}→S2​∣C1​∣=N1​,∣C2​∣=N2​,N1​+N2​=N​

p(yi∣xi)∼p(xi∣yi)p(yi)=p(xi,yi)p(y_i| x_i)\sim p(x_i|y_i) p(y_i) = p(x_i, y_i) p(yi​∣xi​)∼p(xi​∣yi​)p(yi​)=p(xi​,yi​)

yi∼Bernoulli(ϕ)⇒p(yi)=ϕyi(1−ϕ)1−yiy_i\sim Bernoulli(\phi)\Rightarrow p(y_i)=\phi^{y_i} (1-\phi)^{1-y_i} yi​∼Bernoulli(ϕ)⇒p(yi​)=ϕyi​(1−ϕ)1−yi​

{xi∣yi=1∼N(μ1,Σ)xi∣yi=0∼N(μ2,Σ)⇒N(μ1,Σ)yi⋅N(μ2,Σ)1−yi\begin{cases} \begin{array}{l} {x_i | y_i=1 \sim N\left(\mu_{1}, \Sigma\right)} \\\\ {x_i | y_i=0 \sim N\left(\mu_{2}, \Sigma\right)} \end{array} \end{cases} \Rightarrow N(\mu_1, \Sigma)^{y_i} \cdot N\left(\mu_{2}, \Sigma\right)^{1-y_i} ⎩⎨⎧​xi​∣yi​=1∼N(μ1​,Σ)xi​∣yi​=0∼N(μ2​,Σ)​​⇒N(μ1​,Σ)yi​⋅N(μ2​,Σ)1−yi​

对数似然：

L(θ)=log⁡∏i=1Np(xi,yi)=∑i=1N[log⁡N(μ1,Σ)yi⎵(1)+log⁡N(μ2,Σ)1−yi⎵(2)+log⁡ϕyi(1−ϕ)1−yi⎵(3)]θ^=arg⁡max⁡θL(θ)θ=(μ1,μ2,Σ,ϕ)\begin{aligned} \mathbb{L}(\theta) &=\log \prod_{i=1}^N p\left(x_i, y_i\right) \\\\ &=\sum_{i=1}^N \left[\underbrace{ \log N\left(\mu_1, \Sigma\right)^{y_i}}_{(1)}+\underbrace{\log N(\mu_2, \Sigma)^{1-y_i}}_{(2)}+\underbrace{\log \phi^{y_i}(1-\phi)^{1-y_i}}_{(3)}\right]\\\\ \hat{\theta} &=\arg\max _{\theta}\mathbb{L}(\theta) \\\\ \theta &=\left(\mu_1, \mu_2, \Sigma, \phi\right) \end{aligned} L(θ)θ^θ​=logi=1∏N​p(xi​,yi​)=i=1∑N​⎣⎡​(1)logN(μ1​,Σ)yi​​​+(2)logN(μ2​,Σ)1−yi​​​+(3)logϕyi​(1−ϕ)1−yi​​​⎦⎤​=argθmax​L(θ)=(μ1​,μ2​,Σ,ϕ)​
