降维-概率PCA

发表于 2020-01-02 | 分类于 学习

定义

x \in \mathbb{R}^p, \quad z \in \mathbb{R}^q, \quad q< p $$ $$x:\ \text{observed data}, z:\ \text{latent variable}

线性高斯模型：
\begin{cases}
z \sim N\left(0, I_q\right) \\
x=wz+\mu+\varepsilon \\
\varepsilon \sim N\left(0, \sigma^{2} I_{p}\right)
\end{cases}

求z,x∣z,x,z∣xz, x|z, x, z|xz,x∣z,x,z∣x

  1. x∣zx|zx∣z

E[x∣z]=E[wz+μ+ε]=wz+μVar⁡[x∣z]=Var⁡[wz+μ+ε]=σ2Ix∣z∼N(wz+μ,σ2I)\begin{aligned} &E[x | z]=E[w z+\mu+\varepsilon]=w z+\mu\\\\ &\operatorname{Var}[x | z]=\operatorname{Var}[w z+\mu+\varepsilon]=\sigma^{2} I\\\\ &x | z \sim N\left(wz+\mu, \sigma^{2} I\right) \end{aligned} ​E[x∣z]=E[wz+μ+ε]=wz+μVar[x∣z]=Var[wz+μ+ε]=σ2Ix∣z∼N(wz+μ,σ2I)​

  1. z∣xz|xz∣x
    \begin{aligned}
    \operatorname{cov}(x, z) &=E\left[(x-\mu)(z-0)^{T}\right] \\
    &=E\left[(x-\mu) z^T \right]\\
    &=E\left[(w z+\varepsilon) z^{T}\right] \\
    &=E\left[w z z^{\top}+\varepsilon z^T \right] \\
    &= wE\left[zz^T\right] + E \left[ \varepsilon \right] E \left[ z^T \right] \\
    &= w
    \end{aligned}

\begin{aligned}
E[x]&=E\left[w z+\mu+\varepsilon\right]=E\left[w z+\mu\right]+E[\varepsilon]=\mu \\
\operatorname{Var}[x] &=\operatorname{Var}\left[w z+\mu+\varepsilon\right]=\operatorname{Var}[w z]+Var [\varepsilon]\\
&= w \cdot I \cdot w{\top}+\sigma{2} I=w w{\top}+\sigma{2} I
\end{aligned}

(xz)∼N([μ0],[wwT+σ2IwwTI])\left(\begin{array}{l} {x} \\\\ {z} \end{array}\right) \sim N\left( \left[\begin{array}{l} {\mu} \\\\ {0} \end{array}\right],\left[\begin{array}{ll} {ww^T + \sigma^2 I} & {w} \\\\ {w^T} & {I} \end{array}\right] \right) ⎝⎛​xz​⎠⎞​∼N⎝⎛​⎣⎡​μ0​⎦⎤​,⎣⎡​wwT+σ2IwT​wI​⎦⎤​⎠⎞​

直接套用补充的公式可以得到 z∣xz|xz∣x的分布。
